At war with the sun

He races against time

or perhaps he races

against himself

Because

What's the
Difference

Anyway

Evermore he presses on

Knuckles white

with conviction

or perhaps

With Rage

Because

What's the
Difference

Anyway

He is overflowing from

the souls poured into his life

his eyes fill with tears

from physical pain

or perhaps from

Emotional pain

Because

What's the
Difference

Anyway

His sight becomes blurred

he is no longer

able to discern

Wrong from Right

or perhaps

his desires

from his passions

Because

What's the
Difference

Anyway

Flailing into the sun,

he lashes out

hoping to open

the vein of God

or perhaps his own vein

Because

What's the
Difference

Anyway

Engulfed in flames,

embraced by light

He feels at one

with everything

or perhaps

completely forlorn

Because...
